## 💡 Key Actionable Recommendations
1. **Optimize Logistics:** Delivery times in states like **Rio de Janeiro (RJ)** and **Rio Grande do Sul (RS)** average 15 days. Logistics partnerships here need review.
2. **Improve Retention:** The repeat customer rate is only **3.12%**. Targeted loyalty campaigns should focus on "Potential Loyalists" to encourage repeat buying.
3. **Staffing for Peak Hours:** Orders are highly concentrated between **10:00 AM and 4:00 PM**. Operations and support should be fully staffed during these hours.
